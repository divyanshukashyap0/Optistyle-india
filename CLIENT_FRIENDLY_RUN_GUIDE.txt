
================================================================================
CLIENT GUIDE - Managing Your OptiStyle Website
================================================================================

Welcome! This simple guide explains how to manage your online store.

================================================================================
1. LOGGING IN AS ADMIN
================================================================================

1. Open your website.
2. Go to the "Login" page.
3. Enter your Admin Email and Password.
4. Once logged in, go to your Profile (User Icon).
5. Click "Admin Dashboard".

================================================================================
2. PROCESSING ORDERS
================================================================================

WHEN A NEW ORDER ARRIVES:
1. You will receive an email notification.
2. Go to Admin Dashboard -> "Orders".
3. You will see the new order with status "Pending" or "Processing".

SHIPPING THE ITEM:
1. Pack the glasses.
2. In the dashboard, change the Order Status to "Shipped".
3. Hand it over to your courier.

COMPLETING THE ORDER:
1. Once the customer receives it, change status to "Delivered".
2. If it was a Cash on Delivery (COD) order, ensure you collected the money first.

================================================================================
3. MANAGING PRODUCTS
================================================================================

ADD NEW GLASSES:
1. Go to Admin Dashboard -> "Products".
2. Click "Add Product".
3. Upload the image link, enter the name, price, and description.
4. Click Save. It appears on the shop immediately.

CHANGE PRICE:
1. Find the product in the list.
2. Click the "Edit" (pencil) icon.
3. Update the price.
4. Click Save.

================================================================================
4. HANDLING REFUNDS
================================================================================

If a customer asks for a refund:
1. Go to "Refund Requests" in the dashboard.
2. Review their reason.
3. Click "Approve" to refund them (Automatic for Online payments).
4. Click "Reject" if the return is invalid.

================================================================================
5. EMERGENCY CONTROLS
================================================================================

If something goes wrong (e.g., you run out of stock or close for holidays):
1. Go to "System" settings in the dashboard.
2. You can disable "Checkout" to stop people from buying.
3. You can enable "Maintenance Mode" to close the site temporarily.

================================================================================
